A cleaner way to buy for Western Canada.

Stonehouse Dry Supply is based in Calgary, Alberta and built around a simple idea: good brands, clear communication, and a lineup that makes sense for real buyers.

We focus on non-alcoholic beer that earns its shelf space and give retailers, restaurants, bars, and specialty shops a direct, uncomplicated way to order.

From Calgary, we are building a tighter, more reliable wholesale option for Western Canada — without the clutter, noise, or bloated portfolios.
